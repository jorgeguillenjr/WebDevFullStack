Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our program.

Do I need prior coding experience?

+

No prior experience is required. Our curriculum starts from the absolute basics and gradually builds up to advanced concepts. We've designed the course specifically with beginners in mind.

How much time should I commit weekly?

+

For optimal results, we recommend dedicating 15-20 hours per week to the course. This includes watching lectures, completing exercises, working on projects, and participating in mentorship sessions.

What kind of support will I receive?

+

Depending on your plan, you'll have access to our community forum, regular office hours, and 1-on-1 mentorship sessions. Our instructors and teaching assistants are committed to helping you succeed.

Will I build a portfolio during the course?

+

Absolutely! You'll complete 8 projects throughout the course, culminating in a capstone project. These projects are designed to showcase different skills and will form a strong portfolio to present to potential employers.

How does the job guarantee work?

+

For Career Track students, if you don't secure a job within 6 months of completing the program while actively applying and following our career guidance, we'll refund your tuition. Full details are provided in the enrollment agreement.

Can I pay in installments?

+

Yes, we offer monthly payment plans for our Premium and Career Track options. We also partner with several financing providers to make the course more accessible.
